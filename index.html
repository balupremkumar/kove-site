Who owns the code?

You do. Source code goes in your GitHub repo. Credentials and API keys go in your accounts. Hosting is on your bill. When we're done, you could never speak to me again and it would keep running. No retainer lock-in by design.

What if it breaks?

Every project has a 60-day warranty — I fix anything that breaks at no charge. Clients on a monthly retainer get same-day response. I also write proper docs and keep the stack plain, so your team can handle small things themselves.

Will this replace my staff?

No. I build tools that handle the dull, repetitive work. Your team does less data entry and more of the work that actually needs a human.

How long does it take?

A Workflow Review wraps up in one session. A full build is 4–8 weeks from kick-off to handover. I don't drag it out — I'd rather ship something working and iterate than spend months on a spec document.
