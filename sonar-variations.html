Brand · Sonar variations

Five takes on Sonar.

You liked Sonar — depth soundings reading the shape of a cove. Here are five directions, each with motion where it adds something: a radar ping, a rotating sweep, travelling ripples. Each still reads cleanly as a static logo and survives down to favicon size.

VARIATION 01

Radar

// concentric rings, centred — the cleanest logo

Centred concentric rings around a pulsing core. By centring it, the RSS/wifi read disappears entirely — this is pure radar. The most balanced and timeless of the set; works as a perfect circle in any lockup.

Animation: a ping ring expands out from the core every ~1.4s while the core gently breathes. Loops forever, calm.

VARIATION 02

Sweep

// radar screen, scanning — the most alive

The radar rings plus crosshairs, with a luminous sweep arm rotating around the dial. It says actively scanning, finding opportunities — perfect for a brand whose whole pitch is "I find what's slowing you down." Best as a hero/loader; the static version drops the sweep.

Animation: a soft conic sweep rotates ~every 4.4s, catching each ring as it passes — like a real sonar display.

VARIATION 03

Ripple

// sun over a bay — the warmest, most 'cove'

A point of light above downward-opening arcs — sun over the surface of a sheltered bay, ripples spreading out beneath. The friendliest, most human read, and the clearest tie to the literal meaning of Kove.

Animation: the ripples rise and settle in sequence, top to bottom — water gently lapping. The sun breathes.

VARIATION 04

Sounding

// the original, refined — directional & bold

The corner-anchored Sonar you first liked, kept but sharpened: bolder weights, a real focal core, and a ping so it doesn't sit still. The arcs radiate from one point — a sounding sent out into the deep. Distinctive and confident.

Animation: a ping pulses out from the core along the arcs every ~3s. Subtle, premium, never distracting.

VARIATION 05

Beacon

// Sonar in an app tile — most app-native

The radar rendered as ink on a solid gradient tile — the most modern, iOS-native shape. The rings sit knocked-out of the colour so it reads instantly on a home screen or in an app switcher, and pings on launch.

Animation: a ping ripples out across the tile every ~1.4s — ideal as an app-launch or favicon micro-animation.
