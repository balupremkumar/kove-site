FAQ

Questions I get asked, answered straight.

How quickly can you start?

Usually 2–4 weeks from Workflow Review to first build day.

Do you work onsite?

In Canterbury, yes — happy to spend a day with your team. Elsewhere, video and async.

What if I don't like what you build?

First milestone is 50% on signing, 50% on acceptance. If you don't accept, you don't pay the second half.

Can I see examples?

See the work. Most are anonymised because clients prefer it.

Retainers without an Audit + Build first?

Rarely. Retainers work when there's already infrastructure to maintain.

Do I need to be technical?

No. You describe the problem in plain language — I handle everything technical and hand it back running.

What if my data is sensitive?

I can build entirely local, air-gapped systems where nothing leaves your machine. No cloud APIs, no third-party data handling. Common requirement for healthcare and legal clients.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is discussed after the Workflow Review once I understand the scope. The Review itself is a fixed flat fee — book one and you'll have a number in hand before committing to anything further.

How long does a build actually take?

Straightforward automations: 2–3 weeks. Custom tools with multiple integrations: 4–8 weeks. I'll give you a realistic timeline after scoping.

What happens after you hand over?

You get the source code, documentation, and 30 days of support included. After that, retainer or ad hoc — your call.
