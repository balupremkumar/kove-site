Three marks for Kove.

Every concept is built from one idea — Kove ≈ cove: a sheltered bay, a safe harbour for your business's operations. No generic "tech" glyphs. Each works as a mark, a wordmark, an app icon, and in one ink colour.

PaletteDeep Cove
TypeSchibsted Grotesk + JetBrains Mono
FeelPremium · cool · cinematic
CONCEPT 01

Haven

// the sheltering harbour

An almost-complete ring with a single opening at the top — the mouth of the cove — cradling a luminous vessel that sits safe inside. It says the thing the business is about: your operations, sheltered. Confident, minimal, unmistakable at any size — the Apple-grade option.

CONCEPT 02

Tide

// depth contours of an inlet

Stacked contour lines, like the depth chart of a cove on a nautical map — each layer a step closer to the safe centre. It reads as precision, mapping, and getting to the bottom of how your business actually works. The most ownable and the richest for animation.

App icon

Motion: contours ripple outward in sequence like a tide; they gently undulate forever and parallax on scroll.
CONCEPT 03

Keel

// the K, cut by a cove

A precise geometric K where the lower leg isn't a straight stroke — it's a curved sweep, like the inner shore of a cove (or the keel of a boat that's found harbour). The most "logo-like" and corporate of the three; razor-sharp as a tiny favicon, strong on a business card.
