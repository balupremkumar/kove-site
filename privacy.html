Who we are

Kove is an AI automation consultancy operated by Balu Premkumar, based in Christchurch, Canterbury, New Zealand. You can reach us at balu@kove.nz.

This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand) and the Information Privacy Principles (IPPs) contained in it.

What information we collect

We only collect information you give us directly:

Contact form and landing pages — name, email address, phone number (optional), business name (optional), and the message you write. We use this to reply to your enquiry.

Email correspondence — any information you include when you email us directly.

Website analytics — if Cloudflare Web Analytics is active, we collect aggregate, privacy-preserving traffic data (pages visited, browser type, country). This data is not tied to individual users and contains no cookies or fingerprinting.

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other third-party tracking that identifies individuals.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

— respond to your enquiry or booking request
— send you information you've asked for about our services
— fulfil any contract we enter into with you

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not add you to any mailing list without your explicit consent.

Storage and security

Contact form submissions are delivered by email via Resend (a transactional email service) to balu@kove.nz. Your data is processed in transit through Cloudflare's global network and Resend's infrastructure, then stored in our email account. We do not maintain a separate marketing database.

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from loss, misuse, or unauthorised access consistent with IPP 5.

Retention

We keep email correspondence for as long as the business relationship is active or as required for legal and tax purposes, and no longer than 7 years after the relationship ends, consistent with New Zealand tax record-keeping requirements.

Your rights

Under the Privacy Act 2020 you have the right to:

Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you (IPP 6).
Correction — ask us to correct information that is wrong or out of date (IPP 7).
Complaint — if you believe we have breached the Act, you can complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

To exercise any of these rights, email balu@kove.nz with the subject line "Privacy request". We will respond within 20 working days as required by the Act.

Cookies
