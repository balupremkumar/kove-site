MARK 01

Sonar

// depth soundings — Tide, evolved & technical

Bold concentric soundings radiating from a solid core — a sonar reading the depth of a cove. It's the Tide idea with confidence: heavier strokes, a real focal point, and a built-in tech meaning — I read how your business actually works, then map it.

The hook: reads as sonar, ripples, and sound-bars — motion baked in. The arcs animate outward like a ping on load.

Inlet

// a harbour carved from the tile — bold & app-native

A solid gradient squircle with a crescent harbour carved out of the negative space and a single vessel moored inside. The most modern, iOS-native shape of the three — it looks like an app you'd already have on your phone.

The hook: the bite-of-an-Apple move, but it forms a sheltered bay. Bold enough to read at 16px, distinctive enough to stick.

MARK 03

Strata

// layered depth of the bay — Tide, made dimensional

The depth contours of a cove rendered as solid stacked bands — a cross-section of the bay, lightest at the surface, deepest below, with a sun resting above. The richest, most premium of the three; it has weight and dimension the line version never could.

The hook: topographic layers — the same language as maps, data and depth. Premium, calm, and unmistakably about understanding what's beneath.
